## Creative Tone Rules

**Always:**
- Lead with the problem or stat — *then* introduce the product
- Show the actual product clearly (close-up or in context)
- Include the FSSAI/NABL badge or mention in copy for cold audiences
- Use `#08a045` green as the dominant brand color in overlays, text, or backgrounds
- Keep copy minimal — More Green's voice is direct, not verbose

**Never:**
- Before/after body transformation imagery
- Overcrowded layouts with too much text
- Generic wellness stock photos (white woman meditating, green smoothie on marble)
- Artificial food colors or styled shots that don't look real
- Unverified health claims ("cures", "treats", "reverses") — stick to nutrition facts

## Creative Refresh Rule

Meta creative fatigue hits in **2–4 weeks** at starter budgets. When CTR drops >20% from baseline or frequency exceeds 3.0 on cold audiences:

1. Keep the winning pillar (e.g. Problem-aware)
2. Change **one** element: new hook line, new visual, new voiceover
3. Don't rebuild from scratch — iterate

Track which pillar converts best. Double the winning pillar, retire the weakest.
